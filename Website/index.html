About Us

The aim of City 5 Cadet Corp is to instil discipline in all members and develop a better version of each individual through training and leadership.

Corps Superintendent

Overall Commanding Officer

Responsible for overall leadership, discipline, planning and coordination of all cadet activities across the entire Corps. Oversees all divisions, staff officers, and ensures standards are maintained.

Leadership Structure

Corps Sergeant Major

Maintains discipline and drill standards across the corps.

Corps Nursing Officer

Oversees first aid and casualty simulation training.

Staff Officer - Administration

Handles records, planning and coordination.

Staff Officer - Operations

Manages inspections and field activities.

Staff Officer - Training

Organises cadet training programs and drills.

Staff Officer - Youth

Leads youth development programs.

Staff Officer - Dolphins

Oversees Dolphin unit training and activities.
